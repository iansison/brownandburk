 Microlabs Ltd

Microlabs is the Sole Supplier of Brown & Burk Philippines

Brown & Burk Philippines exclusively partners with Microlabs, an esteemed Indian pharmaceutical company, as its sole supplier. Renowned for its commitment to quality and innovation, Microlabs ensures a reliable source of high-quality pharmaceutical products for Brown & Burk's operations in the Philippines.

Microlabs: A Pillar of Excellence in Pharmaceutical Innovation and Manufacturing

Microlabs stands as a distinguished pharmaceutical leader, boasting 15 cutting-edge manufacturing facilities strategically positioned across India. The Bommasandra plant, acknowledged as one of the nation's largest API facilities, upholds rigorous international standards, including USFDA, MHRA, and TGA. Beyond API production, Microlabs' regulated plants in Goa, Veerasandra, Bommasandra, Hosur ML03, and Kumbalgodu excel in diverse pharmaceutical areas, earning approvals from UK MHRA, Health Canada, and US FDA.

This expansive network not only reflects Microlabs' commitment to innovation, quality, and global compliance but also highlights its exceptional prowess as a creator of superior medicines. Microlabs is renowned for its dedication to advancing healthcare solutions, solidifying its reputation as a trusted and influential player in the pharmaceutical landscape.
